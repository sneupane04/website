About Me

Photographer

Self Portrait

Hi, I'm Suren Neupane, a passionate professional photographer based in the sunny state of Florida. With over four years of experience, I specialize in capturing unique and special moments through the lens, particularly in portrait photography. I work closely with individuals and families to create timeless, memorable images that truly reflect their essence.

My photography style is characterized by its natural, candid, and authentic approach. I aim to capture the true personality and spirit of my subjects, providing each client with a personalized experience tailored to their specific needs and preferences.

As a dedicated photographer, I'm always seeking new challenges and opportunities to push my creative boundaries. Whether it's the stunning beauty of nature or the unique personality of an individual, I strive to deliver high-quality, captivating images that exceed expectations.

If you're interested in booking a photoshoot, I'd love to hear from you! Let's discuss your vision and schedule your upcoming shoot. Thank you for considering me as your photographer, and I look forward to capturing your special moments.
